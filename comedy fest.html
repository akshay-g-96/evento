ABOUT


Comedy Wagon is back with the 2nd edition of the Bengaluru Comedy Festival scheduled from November 9th to 13th, 2016 at UB City. 5 days, showcasing 50+ home-brewed and international artists across 2 stages, we are set to raise the happiness index in the city yet again.

The festival will bring together comedians from across the country and beyond to showcase live Stand-up, Improv and Musical comedy. It gets more interesting with panel discussions, workshops, a live podcast, fan jams, art installations, after-parties along with drinking and dining options at the festival area. So, gang up, make plans and get your laughing pants on!
