ABOUT


Drag Fest 2016 - be there or be spared, has been awaited for over a decade at the Jakkur Aerodrome, and is a platform for all the adrenaline junkies from across the nation, to showcase their machines and their talent in their run against time on a quarter mile. The events are spectacles of color and speed, chrome and flash, and ingenuity and engineering.

Drag racing is unique among motor-sports because fans have direct access to the teams, watching from as close as 5 or 10 feet as the highly skilled mechanics ''twirl the iron''.

Some of the most frantic action takes place in the first 30 minutes after a car returns to the pits. If you want a front-row seat to watch the teams at their best, head for the pits a little early. If theres a major engine meltdown on the track and you dont mind missing the rest of the action, you probably wont have to fight for elbow room. The lack of crowds also provides a good chance to snag some autographs at other pit areas.

From the primitive 2 strokes, with their deafening ability, to the sophisticated super-bikes from across the country, along with cars that need such an arena to test their limit, are expected to participate. 

Dont miss this exciting event!
